Play Your Way to a Healthier Mind

Cheza 4 Afya Akili is an AI-powered, gamified mental wellness platform helping children and adolescents across Kenya, Tanzania, and Rwanda build emotional resilience — through play, story, and culture.

Why it works

Built for how children actually learn

AI that adapts to your child

Every child's journey adjusts in real time to their pace, needs, and emotional state, so support feels personal, not generic.

Learning hidden inside play

Story-driven games turn emotional skills into adventure — something kids want to return to, not something they're told to do.

Rooted in African storytelling

Designed around the oral traditions and lived context of East Africa, not adapted from a foreign template.

Built for access, not just ability to pay

Bringing mental health support to children and families who may have little or no access to traditional care.

The journey

From first tap to lasting skill

01

Create a character

A child builds their own character and steps into an interactive story world made for them.

02

Play and check in

As they play, the AI reads their progress and emotional state, adjusting challenges and gentle check-ins along the way.

03

Build real skills

Coping tools and emotional vocabulary grow with every session — skills that last long after the game is closed.
