The problem

Youth need support before crisis point.

Many children and adolescents face emotional stress, anxiety, stigma, and limited access to youth-friendly mental health support. Cheza 4 Afya Akili is designed to make early support more accessible through play.

Rising mental health crisis

Young people increasingly face anxiety, depression, stress, and substance-abuse risks.

Limited youth-friendly support

Support services can be costly, stigmatized, hard to access, and not designed for children.

Gaps in early detection

Many young people receive help only when problems have already become serious.

Digital and cultural gaps

Many tools are not built around African youth realities, languages, stories, and communities.

The solution

A gamified wellness platform built for African children and adolescents.

Cheza 4 Afya Akili uses play, storytelling, AI, and emotional learning to make healing accessible, engaging, and culturally relevant. Instead of waiting for crisis, the platform helps young users check in, express emotions, learn coping skills, and grow over time.

Check-in

Daily emotional mood tracking using text, voice, icons, and child-friendly prompts.

AI personalization

Content adapts based on emotional state, learning pace, and user needs.

Play-based healing

Games, stories, quests, and challenges turn emotional learning into adventure.

Growth pathways

Users unlock coping skills, mentorship routes, and development opportunities.

Games

Play-based healing adventures for young minds.

The platform separates content for children and adolescents so that each age group receives support in a way that feels natural, safe, and age-appropriate.

💛 Children 6–12

Emotion Explorer Adventures

Children learn feelings through characters and simple stories that build emotional literacy.

🌬️ Children 6–12

Calm Down Quests

Breathing and calming mini-games help children manage worry and anxiety.

🏫 Children 6–12

School Life Missions

Safe scenario games help children practice decisions around school, friendship, and bullying.

🎙️ All users

Safe Voice Stories

AI turns feelings into stories, helping users express emotions when words are difficult.

🧭 Teens 13–19

Identity Quest Lines

Life storytelling journeys help adolescents explore self-worth and personal identity.

🤝 Teens 13–19

Peer Healing Circles

Anonymous group healing missions reduce isolation and encourage healthy peer support.

Why Cheza 4 Afya Akili

Built FOR Africa, not imported to Africa.

The platform is rooted in African culture, local languages, oral storytelling, community healing, and real-life situations children face at home, school, and in friendships.

African storytelling

Stories and characters are designed around local culture and lived realities.

Local languages

Designed for Swahili, Sheng, Kinyarwanda, and future African languages.

Community healing

Peer quests and group missions reflect the importance of community support.

Mobile-first access

Built to scale digitally across schools, homes, NGOs, and youth programs.

Business model

Sustainable revenue while keeping core access free.

Cheza 4 Afya Akili can serve children freely at the basic level while generating revenue through premium features, schools, NGOs, and government-supported youth programs.

Freemium

Premium

Schools

NGOs/Governments

Scalability
